About Us

Who are we? What is Makan Nusantara? we born from a community of Indonesian, Malaysian, and Singaporean in nordic countries who missed our food back home. With Makan Nusantara, we don't just cure our home sickness with our food, but to Introduce our culture to our Nordic friends. Our food is Halal certified from multiple muslim community in Europe. We will also listen to our customer for their own preferences.
